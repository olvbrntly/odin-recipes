Krabby Patty

Description

The Krabby Patty is the best-known item at the Krusty Krab and the most famous food in Bikini Bottom. It is a meatless sandwich of sorts made from seaweed-sea buns, undersea vegetables, condiments, and a patty

*This recipe makes 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 1 frozen Krabby Patty
  • Bun
  • Sea Cheese
  • Sea pickles
  • Sea lettuce
  • Sea Tomatoes
  • Sea Onions
  • Tartar Sauce
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Mayonnaise
  • Relish
  • Lots of love

Directions

  1. Gather all of your ingredients to build your Krabby Patty. The above list is the ingredients included in the recommended build.
  2. Slice your sea tomatoes, sea pickles,and Sea onions, Chop your sea lettuce.
  3. Cook your krabby patty according to the instructions on the box.
  4. Lay a piece of sea cheese on top of the Krabby Patty shortly before its finished cooking to allow it to melt
  5. Grab your top and bottom bun.
  6. On the inside of the buns add your condiments, ketchup, mustard, mayonnaise, and relish, preferebly in the shape of smiley faces,
  7. Place your cooked Krabby Patty on top of the bottom bun.
  8. Next, add 3-5 sea pickles
  9. Add two slices of sea onions on top of the sea pickles
  10. PLace two slices of sea tomatoes on the sea onions
  11. Add two pieces of sea lettuce on the top of the sea tomatoes
  12. Top off the Krabby Patty with the top bun
  13. Enjoy

Unfortunately, the Krabby Patty secret sauce remains a mystery. In order to get the full Krabby Patty experience we recommend ordering directly from the Krusty Krab
