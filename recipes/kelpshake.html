Klep Shake

Description

Kelp Shake is a highly popular and addictive drink that comes is a light green bottle with a yellow lid and straw. While the authorities no longer allow Kelp Shake to be sold at restaurants, this is a great alternative you can make at home.

*This recipe makes 1 serving

Ingredients

  • 400ml Kelp Juice
  • 4 ml of radioactive material
  • Ice
  • Blender

Directions

  1. Combine the kelp juice, radioactive material, and ice in the blender
  2. Blend for 1 minute
  3. Pour into your cup and enjoy
