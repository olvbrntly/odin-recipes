Sal-ad

Description

The sal-ad is a menu item added when Pearl redecorates the Krusty Krab as the Kuddly Krab.It is also served at the Krusty Krab when Mr. Krabs opens a salad bar as an attempt/gimmick to attrack more customers

*This recipe makes 2 servings

Ingredients

  • Two slices of sea tomatoes
  • Two pieces of lettuce

Directions

  1. Begin by laying the two pieces of lettuce side by side
  2. Place each slice of sea tomatoe on top of the lettuce
  3. Enjoy?
